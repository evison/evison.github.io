Biography

My name is Yongfeng (Evison) Zhang. I'm now proceeding for my master's degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology (DCST) at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Min Zhang, and majoring in Machine Learning and Personalization theories and technologies. Prior to this, I've obtained my bachelor's degree in July 2011, also from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. Aside from Computer Science and Mathematics, I'm also quite interested in the research of Philosophy of Science.
